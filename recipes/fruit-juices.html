Cranberry Sauce with Orange Juice

Description

This cranberry-orange sauce is a nice tart complement to any holiday feast.

This is a classic!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 (12 ounce) package fresh cranberries

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat; stir until sugar is dissolved. Mix in cranberries; cook, stirring occasionally, until cranberries start to pop, about 10 minutes. Transfer to a bowl; sauce will thicken as it cools.
